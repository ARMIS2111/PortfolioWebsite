About Me

I am Archit Mishra, currently a final year student in Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, Bhubaneshwar where I am pursuing Bachelors in Technology Degree in Computer Science and Engineering.

I have worked as a Data Science Intern with Celebal technologies where I acquired various skills like Exploratory data analysis, deploying ml models using flask app and learnt hyperparameter tuning and Natural language processing.

Recently, I have also been learning Data Analysis and Business Intelligence and have created several projects where I used Python, SQL and R for data analytics along with Tableau and Power BI for creating dashboards. One of the most interesting project undertaken by me was based on TaxiFare Prediction. In this project, I Predicted Taxi Fares using Random Forest model and Artificial Neural Networks. The dataset was based on taxi journeys in New York. I calculated value of fares and tips, based on location, date, and time. I cleaned the dataset and visualize it on the map to understand ride hotspots, predict taxi fares using the Machine learning models, displayed the prediction on the map, and analyzed the results.

I have also developed several ETL Pipeline projects using kafka, pyspark and mongoDb. I have a keen interest in generative AI and have created several models by leveraging Generative Adverserial networks. One such project was Image Superresolution project where I enhanced the image's resolution by 4 times so that the details in the image can be seen in a better manner.

Apart from academics, I like reading fiction and thrillers in my free time. I also represented my hostel in the inter hostel volleyball tournament organized in KIIT.
